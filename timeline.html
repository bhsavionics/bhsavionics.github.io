Our Journey

Follow the evolution of BAAP from its inception to becoming a leading aerospace education program

2023

Project Launch

Burak gathered capable leaders to organize the club and establish the foundation for what would become BAAP.

2024

First Meeting

Gave a summary of future plans for the club and outlined the vision for aerospace education and innovation.

2024

Model Rocket Building

Broke club members into groups and built model rockets, providing hands-on experience with aerospace engineering principles.

2024

Beginning of TARC Interviews

Looked at TARC applications and conducted interviews to choose candidates for the American Rocketry Challenge competition.

2024

TARC Acceptances

Broke up new TARC candidates into groups and began preparation for the national rocketry competition.

2024

V1 Rocket

Built early model of V1 using a BT-80, BT-60, and Wooden Balsa Transition, marking our first major engineering achievement.

What's this about?

This is the Battlefield Aeronautics and Avionics Program, coming to you from Battlefield High School in northern Virginia! We are a group of students extremely interested in Computer Science, Physics, Engineering, and more, applying what we learn in projects. We are also active in our local community and like to teach STEM topics at youth events.
