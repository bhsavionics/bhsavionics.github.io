Our Mission

Empowering students through aerospace engineering, STEM education, and community outreach

The Battlefield Aeronautics and Avionics Program (BAAP) is a student-led organization at Battlefield High School dedicated to exploring the frontiers of aerospace engineering and STEM education. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers, scientists, and innovators through hands-on projects, innovative research, and meaningful community engagement.

We believe in learning by doing, pushing boundaries, and making a positive impact in our community through educational outreach and volunteer work.

What's this about?

This is the Battlefield Aeronautics and Avionics Program, coming to you from Battlefield High School in northern Virginia! We are a group of students extremely interested in Computer Science, Physics, Engineering, and more, applying what we learn in projects. We are also active in our local community and like to teach STEM topics at youth events.
