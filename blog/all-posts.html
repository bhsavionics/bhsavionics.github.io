What's this about?

This is the Battlefield Aeronautics and Avionics Program, coming to you from Battlefield High School in northern Virginia! We are a group of students extremely interested in Computer Science, Physics, Engineering, and more, applying what we learn in various projects. We are also active in our local community and enjoy teaching STEM topics at youth events.
