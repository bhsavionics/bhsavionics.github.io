2024 BAAP Interest Meeting

  • 9/12/24

Key Notes

We had an exciting kickoff for the 2024 BAAP year, with students showing tremendous enthusiasm for all things aviation! Whether you're new to the program or a returning participant, we're thrilled to see so many eager minds ready to explore the world of aeronautics.

More intriguing information

The Battlefield Aeronautics and Aviation Program (BAAP) focuses on providing hands-on experiences and technical knowledge in the fields of aeronautics and aviation. This year, we have a range of exciting activities planned, including small scale rockets and rocketry competitions.

At our next meeting, we will be diving into rocketry! Students will have the opportunity to design, build, and launch small-scale rockets. This fun and interactive workshop will introduce the fundamental principles of propulsion and aerodynamics.

Upcoming Rocket-Building Workshop

Date: 09/26/2024 Location: Room Number: 2203

    What to Expect:
  • Learn about the physics behind rocket launches, including thrust, lift, and drag
  • Build your own small rocket using kits provided
  • Test your designs with a launch, and see who can get the highest altitude!

So in conclusion ...

BAAP is more than just an extracurricular activity. It offers students the chance to develop real-world skills, explore exciting careers in aviation, and prepare for college-level programs.
