Launch Highlights

Launch Summary

Our first-ever rocket launch was a memorable day for everyone involved. We launched V1 of our rocket, focusing on altitude and safe recovery. Despite multiple challenges, the team stayed resilient and committed. Here's how our attempts went:

  • 1st Attempt: Rail guides broke before launch.
  • 2nd Attempt: Excessive friction caused the rocket to ignite but not lift off.
  • 3rd Attempt: Success!

Purple Origin reached 943 ft with a max velocity of 98 m/s, marking a successful first launch! We worked from 8 AM - 3 PM, and everyone’s dedication made it possible.

Next Steps

With this launch under our belt, we now have a solid foundation and valuable flight experience. Our next objective is to hit 790 ft with a flight time of 41-44 seconds. This will involve:

  • Fixing the fins for better stability
  • Adjusting mass to optimize trajectory
  • Updating our simulator with actual flight data

Depending on progress with V2 or modifications to V1 (like adding more weight), we’re aiming for another launch next Saturday.

Final Thoughts

Amazing work today, team! From not knowing how to build or launch a rocket to pulling off a successful flight—we’ve grown so much. Let’s use this momentum to keep learning and improving. This is just the beginning!
