Mountain View STEM Night Recap

  • 09/12/24

Great Job at the Mountain View STEM Night!

Our paper rocket activity was a standout success, allowing participants to unleash their creativity while learning about the principles of aerodynamics and propulsion. Watching everyone launch their rockets and cheer for their friends was a joy. The excitement in the air truly highlighted the spirit of exploration that STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) encourages.

Looking Ahead

We are thrilled to announce that this is just the beginning! Expect to see more engaging events like these in the future, where we can continue to inspire and educate our community about the wonders of STEM. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newcomer, there's always something new to learn and explore.
